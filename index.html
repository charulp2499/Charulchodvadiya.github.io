Hello! I’m a researcher passionate about developing innovative solutions in artificial intelligence, computer vision, image processing, healthcare AI, robotics, and defence AI. My work bridges cutting-edge research with real-world applications, aiming to create technologies that address challenges in video analytics, medical diagnostics, human-AI interaction, and autonomous systems.

I have presented my research at esteemed conferences, including ISBI (International Symposium on Biomedical Imaging), AIxPAC- AIxIA, IEEE GlobConET, IEEE INOCON, and NCVPRIPG, where I showcased optimized solutions for medical imaging, thermal imaging, computer vision, and more. I am honored to have received the Best Practical Paper Award at AIxPAC 2023 for my contributions to night-vision face detection using thermal imaging.

I am committed to transforming AI research into practical solutions that can drive innovation across healthcare, robotics, and defence applications. If you share a passion for computer vision, healthcare AI, robotics, or defence AI, let’s connect and collaborate to shape the future of intelligent systems.

Medical Image Segmentation

Processes 3D medical images from various modalities, such as MRI and CT scans. Implemented segmentation in both centralized and decentralized setups with a novel loss function tailored for each approach.

Github Centralized Approach Github De-Centralized Approach

City Space Segmentation

Developed semantic segmentation models for classifying and segmenting urban street scenes from the Cityspace dataset. And achieved 88-94% segmentation accuracy by optimizing model architectures and employing data augmentation techniques.

Low Light Night Vision Face Detection Using Thermal Imaging

Built a face detection module for low-light conditions using Haar-cascade, Dlib, and YOLOv8. And Enhanced detection accuracy by combining multiple models (e.g Dlib with YOLOv8) for improved performance in challenging lighting.

Palm Print Detection & Identification Using Mobile Cameras

Developed proprietary palm print dataset with manual annotation. Pre process through noise removal and kernel filtering. Applied transfer learning with YOLO (v5 and v8), with YOLOv8 outperforming YOLOv5 by 9-13%.
